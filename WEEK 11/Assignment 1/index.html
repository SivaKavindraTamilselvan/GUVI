Kazi Nazrul Islam

The National Poet of Bangladesh

Kazi Nazrul Islam, widely regarded as the Rebel Poet of Bengal, remains an iconic figure in Bengali literature and music. His life journey from humble beginnings to becoming a symbol of resistance and artistic expression is a testament to his enduring legacy. Born on May 24, 1899, in Churulia, West Bengal, India, Kazi Nazrul Islam was the second of three sons and a daughter in a Bengali Muslim Kazi family. His father, Kazi Fakir Ahmed, was the imam and caretaker of the local mosque and mausoleum, while his mother, Zaheda Khatun, was a homemaker.
